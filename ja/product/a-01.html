AS6520AC

TO-220AC

AS6520AC is the leading edge technology from ANJET for the SiC Schottky barrier diodes. AS6520AC adopts the ANJET proprietary innovative technology to archive high efficiency over all load conditions, resulting from a lower figure of merit (QC x VF). AS6520AC has been designed to meet the most stringent application requirements in this voltage range.

  1. Best in class forward voltage (1.35V)
  2. Best in class figure of merit (QC x VF)
  3. High dv/dt ruggedness
  4. Shorter recovery time
  5. Reduced temperature dependence
  6. High speed switching
  7. High surge current capability
  8. System efficiency improvement
  9. System cost and size savings due to the reduced cooling requirements
  10. Enabling higher frequency and increased power density
  11. Qualified for industrial applications according to the relevant tests of JEDEC (J-STD20 and JESD22)
